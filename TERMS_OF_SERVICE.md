## 2. Description of Service

Discord Lite is a free, open-source plugin for Decky Loader that provides Discord voice chat controls on Steam Deck. The Plugin:
- Connects to your local Discord client via Discord's Rich Presence Connection (RPC)
- Enables voice channel management from the Steam Deck Quick Access Menu
- Provides volume controls for voice chat participants
- Syncs your gaming status with Discord
